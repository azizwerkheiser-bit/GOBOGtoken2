How to Buy

Step-by-step

  1. Install MetaMask (browser extension).
  2. Switch network to BNB Smart Chain.
  3. Get USDT on BNB Smart Chain (and a little BNB for gas).
  4. Open the Presale dApp and click Connect.
  5. Enter USDT amount → click Approve (one-time).
  6. Click Buy. After purchase, click Claim when available.
  7. When presale ends, anyone can call Finalize (permissionless).
Always verify contract addresses on the official links below.

FAQ

Is there minting or hidden admin control?

No. The token supply is fixed. The presale is designed to be transparent and minimal.

Are there taxes, blacklists, or pauses?

No. GOBOG is meant to be a simple collectible token (no “weird features”).

What happens to unsold presale tokens?

Unsold presale allocation is burned at finalize.

When can I claim my tokens?

Claiming depends on the presale contract rules. The dApp will show your claimable balance.

Will the price go up?

Price is market-driven. GOBOG is a collectible. There are no profit promises.
